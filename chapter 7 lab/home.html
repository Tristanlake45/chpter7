Quick Facts

The 2020 Honda Ridgeline boasts a lot of interesting stats, such as

  • Engine: 3.5-liter V6 engine with 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque
  • Transmission: Nine-speed automatic
  • Drive: Front-wheel drive standard, all-wheel drive optional
  • Fuel economy: 19/26 mpg city/highway for front-wheel drive, 19/24 mpg for all-wheel drive
  • Towing capacity: 5,000 lbs
